One Of the Greatest Progresive Rock Band

Porcupine Tree have managed to defy genres and blend together numerous ambient, rock and avant-garde styles to create a musical landscape that is both refreshing and compulsively seductive. The hypnotic rhythms, spacy synthesizers, glissando guitar and crazy voices which made the style successful are all contained here.

Steven Wilson

An English musician and record producer, the founder, lead guitarist, singer, and songwriter of the band Porcupine Tree.

Richard Barbieri

An English synthesizer player, keyboardist and composer. he is known as the keyboard player in the progressive rock band Porcupine Tree

Gavin Harrison

Drummer of Porcupine Tree. He is best known for playing with the British progressive rock bands Porcupine Tree and King Crimson.

Colin Edwin

Bass Guitarist of Porcupine Tree. Since December 1993 he has been a member of the British band Porcupine Tree, where he plays both fretted and fretless bass guitar.
